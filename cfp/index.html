Call for Papers

We invite oral, poster and applied contributions at the intersection of biotechnology, food technologies and brewing science.

Submission types

Abstract (1 page)

For the RSCI abstract book. Word + PDF, up to 1 page.

Poster (A1)

A1 poster (RU or EN). Recommended structure: Intro / Methods / Results / Conclusions.

Talk + slides

Oral talk with slides. Timing will be shared after acceptance.

Key dates

Two tracks: conference participation & RSCI abstracts (Track A) and Proceedings (English) under consideration (Track B).

Track A — participation + abstracts (RSCI)

  • 02 Mar 2026 — Abstract / poster submission deadline
  • 06 Mar 2026 — Notification of acceptance
  • 10 Mar 2026 — Final abstract (camera‑ready) deadline
  • 12 Mar 2026 — Preliminary programme online
  • 15 Mar 2026 — Registration deadline (attendance)

Track B — Proceedings (English) • under consideration

  • 20 Mar 2026 — Proceedings submission opens
  • 17 Apr 2026 — Full paper submission deadline
  • 22 May 2026 — First decision (peer review)
  • 12 Jun 2026 — Revised manuscript deadline
  • 26 Jun 2026 — Final decision
  • 10 Jul 2026 — Camera‑ready + author forms

Track B dates may be adjusted after publisher confirmation.
