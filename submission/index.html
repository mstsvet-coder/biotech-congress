Submission & registration

Two clear routes: attend as a participant (no talk) or submit a contribution as an author.

I want to attend (no talk)

  1. Fill in the participant form (6–8 fields).
  2. Receive confirmation by email.
  3. Come to the venue on 17–18 March 2026.
Open participant form

I want to present / publish

  1. Prepare a 1‑page abstract (Word + PDF).
  2. Submit via the materials form.
  3. Wait for review and confirmation from the committee.
Open submission form
Submission workflow:

We plan to manage submissions and peer review using Janeway (editorial management system). Online forms are used for initial intake and routing.

If the forms do not open, use the backup email: biotech-congress@yandex.ru

Key rules (short)
  • Abstracts: up to 1 page.
  • Up to 2 abstracts per author (including co‑authorship).
  • Each abstract is submitted separately: 1 submission = 1 abstract in two formats (Word + PDF).
  • Languages: Russian or English.
