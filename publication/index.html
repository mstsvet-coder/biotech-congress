Submission workflow

Janeway (planned)

We plan to manage submissions and peer review using Janeway (editorial management system). Online forms are currently used for initial data collection and routing.

All submissions receive a unique internal ID and are tracked through screening, review, revision and final decision.

If the form does not open

If the online form is not accessible in your region/network, submit materials via email as a fallback channel:

biotech-congress@yandex.ru

The editorial office will confirm receipt.

Peer review & editorial decision

  • Scope and format screening (editorial office).
  • Similarity screening (to prevent plagiarism and duplicate publication).
  • Peer review (single‑blind or double‑blind depending on the track; details at submission).
  • Decision: accept / minor revision / major revision / reject.
  • Point‑by‑point response to reviewers is expected for revisions.
Evaluation criteria (short list)
  • Novelty and scientific/practical contribution
  • Methodological soundness and reproducibility
  • Clarity of results and conclusions
  • Relevance to conference tracks
  • Quality of references and proper citation
  • Ethical compliance (authorship, originality, data integrity)

Publication ethics

We follow internationally accepted publication ethics principles (authorship transparency, COI disclosure, proper citation, prohibition of plagiarism and fabrication). Authors must confirm originality and data integrity.
