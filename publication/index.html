Publication & review

We aim for a transparent and publisher‑friendly publication workflow: clear scope, review model, and ethics.

Publication channels

RSCI / RINZ abstract book

Accepted 1‑page abstracts will be included in the conference abstract book (RSCI/RINZ).

Journal option (RU)

Selected extended papers may be recommended for submission to the Russian Biotechnological Journal (RJBT).

Proceedings (English) — under consideration

The committee is negotiating an English proceedings volume in an international series. Publication depends on the publisher’s decision.

RJBT: https://rjbt.ru/

Review model

  • Editorial check (scope, format, completeness).
  • Independent review by at least 2 reviewers (as applicable).
  • Editorial selection based on quality, relevance and programme balance.
  • Authors receive instructions for revisions and camera‑ready files (if required).
Evaluation criteria (short list)
  • Novelty and scientific/practical contribution
  • Methodological soundness and reproducibility
  • Clarity of results and conclusions
  • Relevance to conference tracks
  • Quality of references and proper citation
  • Ethical compliance (authorship, originality, data integrity)

Publishing ethics & AI policy

A short, explicit ethics policy is a strong trust signal for publishers. Please read before submitting.
