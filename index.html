About the event

A joint event: the 3rd International Congress “Biotechnology and Food Technologies” and the 1st International applied conference “Brewers’ School”, held in Saint Petersburg on 17–18 March 2026. Participation is free.

Read the official invitation (full text)

The Saint Petersburg State Technological Institute (Technical University), together with the Council for the Development of the Beer and Soft Drinks Industry and ANO “NITs PBO”, invite you to take part in the joint event — the 3rd International Congress “Biotechnology and Food Technologies” and the 1st International applied conference “Brewers’ School”.

The event is dedicated to the 50th anniversary of the Department of Microbiological Synthesis Technology and will take place at one of Russia’s oldest technological universities. Saint Petersburg offers a unique combination of history, innovation and cultural heritage, and is a major educational, research and industrial centre.

Thematic programme (tracks)

A non-exhaustive list of topics. If your work sits at the boundary — submit anyway.

Biotech & food technologies

  • Enzyme and microbial technologies
  • Food quality and safety
  • Hygienic design of food equipment
  • Agricultural & industrial biotechnology
  • Biocatalysts, biofuels, bioinformatics
  • Biomaterials; cellular/molecular biotech
  • Genetics and genetic engineering
  • Environmental & medical biotechnology
  • Microbial physiology, metabolism & gene expression

Brewing & beverages

  • Beer and beverage production technology
  • Raw materials for brewing
  • Technologies and equipment for brewing raw materials
  • Training and workforce development for the brewing industry
