Proceedings (English)

A dedicated track for English full papers. The organising committee is preparing an English‑language proceedings volume and is currently exploring publication opportunities with an international publisher/series.

Publisher confirmation pending • Peer review + editorial selection

Who should submit

  • Authors targeting an international‑level publication (English).
  • Research and applied papers in biotechnology, food science, fermentation, brewing technologies, applied microbiology, and bioengineering.
  • Manuscripts that can pass peer review and editorial selection.

If you only plan to attend, use the participant registration route on the Submission page.

Requirements (quick)

  • Language: English.
  • Length: recommended 6–10 pages (minimum 6 pages).
  • Structure: IMRAD is recommended for research papers.
  • Originality: original work only; no duplicate submission.
  • Figures & permissions: authors must obtain permissions for any third‑party materials.
  • Presentation requirement: accepted papers should be presented on‑site by at least one author.
Peer review statement Ethics & AI policy

Details

Manuscript structure (IMRAD)
We recommend the IMRAD structure (Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion) for research papers. Keep methods reproducible and results supported by data.
Figures, tables and permissions
Use high‑quality figures (readable labels, consistent units). If you reuse any copyrighted images/tables, obtain written permission and cite the source.
References
References must be complete and consistently formatted. Cite relevant prior work and avoid “AI‑hallucinated” references.
After acceptance
After acceptance you will submit a camera‑ready version and (if required) author forms. The editorial office may request language polishing and minor formatting alignment.

Submission workflow

We plan to manage submissions and peer review using Janeway (editorial management system).
