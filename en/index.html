Invitation

Purpose and context

Dear colleagues, The Saint Petersburg State Technological Institute (Technical University), together with the Council for the Development of the Beer and Soft Drinks Industry and the ANO “NITs PBO”, invite you to participate in the joint event of the 3rd International Congress “Biotechnology and Food Technology” and the 1st International Conference of Applied Sciences “School of Brewers,” held in Saint Petersburg, Russia, on March 17–18, 2026.

The event is dedicated to the 50th anniversary of the Department of Microbiological Synthesis Technology at SPbGTI (TU) — the first department to train biotechnologists in the former USSR — and to the opening of the Brewers’ School, continuing the legacy of the Witte School of Brewing founded in 1898.

We invite researchers, scientists, specialists, practitioners, and enthusiasts in biotechnology, food science and technology, and related fields to present their key developments and innovations.

Honorary chairs and leadership (as stated on the source page): Andrey Pavlovich Shevchik (Rector); Taimuraz Kazbekovich Bolloev (Council Chair); Maksim Nikolaevich Novikov (Director of ANO “NITs PBO”); Mark Markovich Shamtsyan (Congress & Conference Chair); Natalia Evgenievna Khudoshina (Conference Co‑Chair).

Saint Petersburg is a major educational, research and industrial center — an inspiring venue for scientific and applied dialogue.

  • Biofuels

  • Biotechnology of microbial enzymes
  • Cellular / molecular biotechnology
  • Genetics and genetic engineering
  • Environmental biotechnology
  • Medical biotechnology
  • Physiology, metabolism and gene expression of microorganisms
  • Plant biotechnology

This statement reflects current intentions; it should be updated once the publisher/format is confirmed.

Abstract formatting guidelines (expand)
  1. Page setup: A4 portrait. Margins: top/bottom 2 cm; left/right 2.5 cm. Headers/footers: 1.25 cm.
  2. Title: no first-line indent. Times New Roman 12 pt, UPPERCASE, bold, centered.
  3. Authors: Times New Roman 12 pt, bold italic, centered. Please underline the presenting author. Format: surname + initials. If multiple affiliations — indicate affiliations.
  4. Affiliation & e‑mail and English block: full affiliation name (italic, centered) + e‑mail. Then repeat title/authors/affiliation in English (Times New Roman 10 pt), then start the abstract text.
  5. Abstract text: TNR 12 pt; single spacing; justified; first-line indent. Disable hyphenation. First-line indent 1.25 cm; spacing before/after 0.
  6. Tables and figures: captions TNR 11 pt bold; leave one empty line before/after. Reference all tables/figures in text. Figure width ≥1000 px; 260–350 dpi.
  7. Formulas: centered.
  8. Funding/support: italic line after the main text before References.
  9. References: at the end; cite in text as [1], [2], … in order of appearance.
  10. Length: maximum 1 page.
  11. Submission: max 2 abstracts per author (including co‑authorship). Submit one abstract per submission in Word + PDF. File name: presenting author surname + section.

Before submitting, please verify the final version agreed by all co-authors.

Poster presentation (expand)
