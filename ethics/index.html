Publishing ethics & AI policy

A short policy to protect authors, reviewers and the publisher.

Core principles

  • Original work only. No plagiarism and no duplicate submission.
  • All listed authors must have made a real contribution and approve the final version.
  • All conflicts of interest must be disclosed.
  • Data and images must be presented honestly (no deceptive manipulation).

AI and large language models

  • AI tools may be used for language polishing and formatting assistance, but the authors remain fully responsible for the content.
  • AI systems cannot be listed as authors.
  • If AI was used beyond spelling/grammar (e.g., generating text, figures, code), disclose it in a short note.
  • Do not submit fabricated references, data, or results (AI can hallucinate).

Review integrity

  • The review process is confidential.
  • Do not attempt to influence reviewers or editors outside the official channels.

How to report issues

If you suspect plagiarism, authorship disputes or data manipulation, contact the organising committee: biotech-congress@yandex.ru.
